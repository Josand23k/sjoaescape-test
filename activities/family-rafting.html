Family Rafting in Sjoa

Come and enjoy it with the whole family!
It is an ideal activity for families with children over 6 years of age. But at the same time, it's also fun enough for even 15 year olds.
This tour lasts half a day (around 4 to 5 hours), which includes time to explain the tour, to prepare with the necessary equipment to carry out the activity (wetsuit, helmet, flotation vest, etc.), Safety talk and how to paddle, the time in the river and time after the rafting to take a shower, if you wish, and change, see the photos and have a snack.
Depending on the level of the river, it is the section of the river where we will practice family rafting, either on the Otta, Sjoa or Lagan river.
The ideal season is from June to September.

It incluides

  • Personal equipment
  • Lunch
  • Certified equipment
  • Specialized Guides
  • Security instructions
  • and too much fun!

What Brig

  • Wool underwear to use in the river
  • Towel
  • Backpack
  • Wool socks to use in the river
  • Medicines if you need them
  • Snack, if you like to eat

Technical data

  • 12 km
  • Intermediate difficulty
  • Jun/Jul High water
  • Does not include photographs
  • Rapids Class III
  • Price 950 NOK

Recommendations:

When you make your reservation, we will contact you, where you can give us more information necessary to provide you with a better experience.
If you have any physical, medical or nutritional condition, we recommend that you let us know in advance.
