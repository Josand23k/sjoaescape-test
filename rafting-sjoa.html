Rafting in Sjoa

On this webpage you can find the different options for rafting in Sjoa.
Here we will describe each of the options, so you can choose the one that best suits your needs.
They are tours to enjoy with friends, family or as a couple. Of course you can customize your trip, depending on the number of participants.
There are four options we have for you:

  • Sjoa Classic
  • Family Rafting
  • Rafting Åseng
  • Ultimate Rafting Sjoa

Find the rafting tour that best suits what you are looking for. Click on the tour that you like, there you can find more information about the tour, what it includes and what we recommend you bring.
Below you can find other activities that you can combine with rafting, as well as lodging options.
